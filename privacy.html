Welcome to Idle Empire (“we,” “our,” or “us”). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our game, Idle Empire (the “Game”).

We value your privacy. Idle Empire is designed to collect as little personal information as possible. We only collect the following types of information:

If you make a purchase in the Game:

  • We do not have access to your full payment details.

See the Stripe Privacy Policy for more details.

b. Advertising Data

We use Unity Ads to display ads within the Game. Unity Ads may collect device identifiers, coarse location, ad interactions, and other usage data to serve personalized or contextual ads.

For more details, see the Unity Privacy Policy.

c. Gameplay Data (Non-Personal)

We may collect minimal anonymous statistics such as:

This data is used only to improve gameplay balance and performance.

We use the limited information we collect for:

  • Processing in-game purchases

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties.

3. Children’s Privacy

Our Game is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from anyone under the age of 13. If you believe we may have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

4. Data Retention

We retain only non-personal game data for gameplay and analytics purposes. Purchase records are securely stored by Stripe in accordance with legal and financial requirements.

5. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or loss. However, no online service is completely secure, and you use the Game at your own risk.
